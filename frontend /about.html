Psi Eteen

I write to figure
things out.

Writing has always been, for me, a form of thinking. Not a record of thoughts I've already had, but a process for arriving at thoughts I couldn't reach any other way. That's what this space is.

I’ve always wondered why we exist in the first place, and I’m still searching for that answer. In the process of chasing that question, I keep uncovering other truths about life and society. I write what I think—and I think a lot. You could call me an overthinker. Here, you’ll find thoughts on psychology, philosophy, world power structures, geopolitics, quantum physics, and modern technology.

I don’t expect anything in return from writing. I just write because I feel I should.

If something here is useful to you, I'm glad. If it starts a conversation, even better.
